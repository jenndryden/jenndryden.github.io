Hackathon Winner Sept – Dec 2021

Ceridian World — Most Innovative Hackathon Winner, AR Mobile Application

Skills: C#, Unity, Figma · View on LinkedIn ↗

During Fall 2021, I interned at Ceridian as a Software Developer for four months. Before wrapping up, I participated in the two-day company-wide hackathon and built an augmented reality onboarding tool using Unity and C#. I was lucky enough to take home the Most Innovative award.

The vision of the project was to make onboarding a more immersive and enjoyable experience. As employees increasingly join companies virtually, it's hard for new hires to feel connected and genuinely explore the company beyond their laptop screen. Ceridian World made that possible through AR.
